My Vision

My vision of website's content is to help change how people think with the term “celebration of life”. Today most people think of the celebration of life as being a postmortem thing. The purpose is to get people thinking and acting with more enthusiasm in celebrating life, theirs and others, more now in the present. This Brand will cover a number of categories, but I am going to start with Birthdays. Getting people more comfortable and excited about celebrating themselves and others on their birthday, getting away from celebrating age. The vision includes creating behavior/habit that on a person's birthday they are celebrating/thanking their parents for bringing them into this world. Another spin on this birthday element, is to encourage people to celebrate the parents of children on the child's birthday. The latter 2 angles, I am in the process of pitching ideas to greeting card companies. Leveraging this website to at least get the premise advertised, even if it is not set up for purchasing/processing would be helpful. The vision of this website / birthday portion (1st or 2nd phase) is to provide unique ways of celebrating peoples birthdays and to provide links where they can go. For example, I know there is a website out there where someone can pay a celebrity to give someone a personal birthday greeting. I am also developing a way to send unique Happy Birthday text messages that are sent to the individual at 12:01 on their birthday. The 12:01 am ensures the sender's birthday wish is the 1st one the birthday person receives. Maybe a website template can be created where I can later change out pictures, text, links, etc.
